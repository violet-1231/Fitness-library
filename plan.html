Your Personalized Fitness Plan

Week 1: Adaptation Phase

Monday: Full-body Light Workout

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes jogging or brisk walking
  • Squats: 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Push-ups (kneeling if needed): 3 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets × 20 seconds
  • Stretching: 5 minutes (focus on legs and core)

Tuesday: Cardio

  • Brisk walk or light jog: 20 minutes
  • Intervals: 1 min fast walk + 1 min slow walk, repeat 8 times
  • Stretching: 5 minutes (focus on calves and hips)

Wednesday: Rest or Light Activity

  • Walk: 15–20 minutes (leisurely pace)
  • Full-body stretching: 10 minutes

Thursday: Full-body Strength

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes (arm circles + leg swings)
  • Lunges: 3 sets × 10 reps per side
  • Dumbbell curls (use water bottles if needed): 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Crunches: 3 sets × 15 reps
  • Stretching: 5 minutes (focus on quads and arms)

Friday: Cardio

  • Cycling or swimming: 25 minutes
  • Core workout: 3 sets × 15 crunches
  • Stretching: 5 minutes (focus on back and shoulders)

Saturday: Full-body Workout

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes (jumping jacks)
  • Squat jumps: 3 sets × 10 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets × max reps (don’t force)
  • Plank: 3 sets × 30 seconds
  • Stretching: 5 minutes (full-body)

Sunday: Rest

  • Rest well and hydrate (drink ≥2L water)
  • Optional: 10 minutes light stretching
