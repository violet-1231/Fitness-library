Fitness Knowledge

Learn to train smarter, not harder

The importance of warm-up exercises

Before engaging in any high-intensity activity, it is necessary to perform a warm-up for at least 5 minutes. This can effectively reduce the risk of injury and activate muscles and joints.

Eating reasonably and exercising

70% of the success of a fitness plan depends on diet, and 30% depends on exercise. Rational intake of protein and carbohydrates can aid muscle recovery.

How to avoid fitness plateaus

When fitness results stagnate, you can try adjusting training intensity, shortening rest time, or changing training movements to break through the bottleneck.

Stretching after fitness training

Proper stretching after exercise can alleviate muscle soreness, promote recovery, and prevent poor posture. Focus on stretching the muscles you used during training.
